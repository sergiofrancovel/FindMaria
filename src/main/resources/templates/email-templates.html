Email Templates

Use the following email templates to contact news outlets, authorities, and other organizations to help spread the word about Maria's disappearance. You can edit the templates as needed and click the copy button to copy the email content.

Template 1: Contacting News Outlets

Subject: Urgent: Missing 12-Year-Old Maria Gomez-Perez

Body:

Dear [News Outlet],

I am writing to inform you about the urgent case of a missing 12-year-old girl, Maria Gomez-Perez, who disappeared on May 29th in Gainesville, GA. Maria was last seen near her home on Westside Drive. She is 5 feet 3 inches tall, weighs between 100 to 110 pounds, and has long black hair and brown eyes. She was wearing a blue t-shirt and black jeans at the time of her disappearance.

Her family and the local community are desperate for her safe return and have been tirelessly working to spread awareness and gather any information that could lead to her whereabouts. Your coverage and support in spreading the word about Maria's disappearance could be crucial in bringing her home safely.

Please find additional details about her case at the following link: Hall County authorities continue search for missing Gainesville girl.

Thank you for your attention and assistance in this matter.

Sincerely,

[Your Name]
