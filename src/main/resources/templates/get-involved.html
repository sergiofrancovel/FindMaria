Get Involved

There are many ways you can help in the search for Maria. Your involvement can make a significant difference in bringing her home safely. Here are some ways you can get involved:

Volunteer for Search Parties

Join local search parties to help find Maria. You can sign up to volunteer and participate in organized search efforts.

Join the "Help Find Maria" Facebook Group

Stay updated and contribute to the search efforts by joining the "Help Find Maria" Facebook group. This Facebook group is essential for sharing updates and coordinating efforts (this page relies heavily on the updates posted on that group). Any contributions to the Facebook page will greatly benefit the search.

Join the Facebook Group

Get Free Yard Signs!

We're offering free yard signs for people to place on their property or a busy location to help spread the word.

However, sometimes we can run out. If you'd like to call ahead if we have any available, call us at 770-297-9550. If we have some, the free yard signs are available at:

Spread Awareness on Social Media

Share information about Maria's disappearance on your social media platforms. Use the hashtag #HelpFindMaria to help spread the word.
