Get Involved

There are many ways you can help in the search for Maria. Your involvement can make a significant difference in bringing her home safely. Here are some ways you can get involved:

Volunteer for Search Parties

Join local search parties to help find Maria. You can sign up to volunteer and participate in organized search efforts.

Spread Awareness on Social Media

Share information about Maria's disappearance on your social media platforms. Use the hashtag #HelpFindMaria to help spread the word.

Donate to Support Search Efforts

Unfortunately, the donate feature is not supported right now. We're working to set up a GoFundMe for the family and the search rescue effort, or to attach here an already existing GoFundMe page. Once we find the proper resouces, there will be a button right below this text that you can click on for donating. If you are aware or already know about an existing GoFundMe page for this family, please share that information here.

Contact Local Authorities

If you have any information about Maria's whereabouts, please contact the Hall County Sheriff's Office or send an anonymous tip through our website.

Email: acrews@hallcounty.org
